How to Start Earning

Getting started with ReliPay is straightforward and designed for your convenience. Follow these steps to begin earning rewards:

  1. Register for a free account using your valid email address.
  2. Log in to your account and navigate to the Start Earning page.
  3. Select from a variety of available tasks, such as watching promotional videos, completing special offers, or participating in surveys.
  4. Earn points upon successful completion of each task, which accumulate in your account.
  5. Redeem your accumulated points for monetary rewards or other valuable incentives.

Why Are We Doing This?

At ReliPay, our mission is to empower users by providing accessible and legitimate opportunities to earn money online. We believe in creating a transparent platform that benefits both users and advertisers by connecting audiences with meaningful tasks and offers. Our goal is to foster a community where individuals can monetize their time effectively, promoting financial inclusion and digital engagement.
